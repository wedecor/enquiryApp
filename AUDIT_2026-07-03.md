## DECISIONS (defaults — product owner may override before you start)

- **D1 — Canonical status vocabulary** (used everywhere): `new`, `contacted`, `in_talks`, `quote_sent`, `approved`, `scheduled`, `completed`, `not_interested`, `closed_lost`, `cancelled`.
  - Legacy → canonical mapping for migration and tolerant reads: `in_progress→in_talks`, `confirmed→approved`, `quoted→quote_sent`, `enquired→new`, `assigned→in_talks`.
  - Categories: active = `new, contacted, in_talks, quote_sent` · won = `approved, scheduled, completed` · lost = `not_interested, closed_lost, cancelled`.
  - Staff transitions: `new→[contacted, in_talks, cancelled, not_interested]`, `contacted→[in_talks, cancelled, not_interested]`, `in_talks→[quote_sent, cancelled, not_interested]`, `quote_sent→[approved, closed_lost, not_interested]`, `approved→[scheduled, cancelled]`, `scheduled→[completed, cancelled]`, terminal: `completed, cancelled, closed_lost, not_interested`. Admin: any→any.
- **D2 — Staff may NOT create enquiries.** Hide all create entry points from staff (rules already deny).
- **D3 — Staff may NOT reassign/unassign enquiries.** Enforce in rules.
- **D4 — Canonical payment vocabulary:** `pending`, `partial`, `paid`, `overdue`. Legacy mapping: `unpaid→pending`, `no_payment→pending`, `advance_paid→partial`, `full_payment→paid`.
- **D5 — Deactivated users:** blocked at sign-in/session level AND in Firestore rules.
- **D6 — Profile visibility:** staff may read colleague names/emails (needed for assignee display); this is accepted. But user pickers/lists must show only active users.
- **D7 — Prod telemetry:** Crashlytics, Analytics, and Performance are all ENABLED in prod builds.
